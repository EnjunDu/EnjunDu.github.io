## 🏆 Xu Teli Scholarship — Highest Honor at BIT

The **Xu Teli Scholarship** was established in honor of **Xu Teli**, a renowned educator and one of the important pioneers in the history of Beijing Institute of Technology. The scholarship represents the university’s highest-level student honor and aims to encourage excellence in scientific research, innovation, and academic leadership.  [oai_citation:0‡Xuteli College](https://xuteli.bit.edu.cn/english/ABOUTSX/index.htm?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

Each year, only a limited number of outstanding students are selected through a highly competitive evaluation and public defense process.

The scholarship emphasizes:

- Academic excellence and GPA performance  
- Research innovation and scientific contributions  
- Leadership and social responsibility  
- Interdisciplinary exploration and future potential  

The award amount for the scholarship is **RMB 50,000**.

## 🎓 2026 Final Defense

On **April 19, 2026**, I participated in the final defense session of the Xu Teli Scholarship at the **Grand Auditorium of BIT Liangxiang Campus**.

During the defense, candidates presented:

- Academic achievements and research experiences  
- Publications, projects, and technical contributions  
- Innovation capability and future research directions  
- Leadership experiences and comprehensive development  

The evaluation committee conducted a rigorous review focusing on both academic performance and long-term research potential.

## 📖 About Xu Teli

Xu Teli was a prominent Chinese educator, revolutionary, and one of the early contributors to science and engineering education in modern China. He also served as an important educational leader during the early development of institutions that later evolved into Beijing Institute of Technology.
